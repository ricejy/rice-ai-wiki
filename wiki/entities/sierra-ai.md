# Sierra AI

## Overview

Enterprise AI platform for customer experience agents. Builds Agent OS — a full stack for deploying conversational AI agents across chat, SMS, WhatsApp, email, voice, and ChatGPT.

## Products

- **Agent Studio** — dashboard for building, deploying, and managing agents across channels
- **Ghostwriter** — an "agent-building agent" that creates production-ready, multilingual agents from SOPs, transcripts, photos, or plain English
- **Insights** — analytics suite: Explorer (deep conversation research), Monitors (proactive issue detection), Experiments (multivariate testing), Observability (action-level visibility)
- **Agent Data Platform** — agent memory, customer data integration, recommendations engine, proactive engagement workflows

## Business model

Outcome-based pricing — charges based on results, not per-message or per-token.

## Customers

Major brands including Rocket Mortgage, Gap Inc., SiriusXM, SoFi, Wayfair across financial services, healthcare, retail, and telecom.

## Relevance

One of the most mature enterprise [[Agent Commerce]] deployments. Unlike [[Anthropic]]'s template-based approach for financial services or [[Google]]'s protocol-level AP2, Sierra provides a full vertical platform specifically for customer-facing agent experiences.
