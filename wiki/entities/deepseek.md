# DeepSeek

## Overview

Chinese AI lab developing open-source LLMs. Notable for pushing efficiency innovations in model architecture.

## Key contributions (in wiki)

- **DeepSeek Sparse Attention (DSA)** — introduced in DeepSeek-V3.2. Two-stage [[Sparse Attention]] pipeline (lightning indexer → targeted attention) that keeps attention cost approximately constant regardless of context length.

## DeepSeek V4

Launched April 2026. Triggered a scramble among Chinese tech firms to secure Huawei AI chips. Signal that the Chinese AI ecosystem is accelerating despite US chip export controls, with Huawei's AI chip sales surging as [[Nvidia]] stalls in China.
