# A2A Protocol

## Overview

The Agent-to-Agent (A2A) protocol, created by [[Google]], enables AI agents to discover and collaborate with other agents across different providers and frameworks. Part of the [[Agent Interoperability]] stack alongside [[Model Context Protocol]] (tools) and AP2 (payments).

AP2 is built as an extension of A2A, adding payment capabilities to agent-to-agent interactions.
